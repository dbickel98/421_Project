bart_lewis

This is a movie sure to please those who fervently believe in the supernatural. All others should be warned that it will assault your intellectual sensibilities. On a technical level the cinematography and sound were certainly adequate. The acting was professional. The sets were attractive. The problem was the basic premise, the screenplay and the pedestrian special effects (perhaps these improved near the end of the film, but I was forced to abandon my viewing when the rubber ball came sailing out of the storage space). Really, if you are not a fan (and staunch believer) of the supernatural, save your money - or rent anything else. It will be better. Movies such as this should be forced to carry a government health warning: Caution, viewing this film my cause drooling, incredulity and momentary loss of your higher mental capabilities
